About PhosPredict AI

This project was developed during our internship at OCP Group in August 2023.

This innovative project takes the form of a meticulously crafted web application, implemented through the utilization of an advanced machine learning model. Its primary objective is to provide you with an accurate insight into predictions regarding global phosphate prices for the year of your choice. What's more, this technological feat is capable of extending its reach to the furthest horizons of the future.

Imagine being able to visualize potential fluctuations in phosphate prices, guide your decisions, and plan accordingly. Drawing from historical data and current trends, you'll have the ability to anticipate price variations and make informed actions.

However, that's not all. This application goes far beyond simple price prediction. It also incorporates a sophisticated module that takes into account a variety of factors to forecast phosphate production quantities in your selected country. These factors encompass not only nitrogen and potash production but also critical elements such as population growth and fertilizer consumption in each country. This holistic approach leads to even more precise and meaningful predictions, providing a comprehensive understanding of market dynamics.

All that's left is for you to seize this exciting opportunity by trying it out for yourself. Simply select the name of the country that interests you and watch the predictions unfold. With this application, the future of the phosphate market has never been clearer. Get ready to explore data, make informed decisions, and open new perspectives in the exciting field of price prediction and phosphate production.
